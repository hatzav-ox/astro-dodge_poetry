Near Earth Objects

A list of current space objects that have the closest orbit.

To better observe and track Near Earth Objects(NEOs), users can create watchlists of objects that are of interest. By adding an entry to a profile watchlist, users track recent NEOs that passes within a certain distance of Earth or is otherwise of scientific interest. An unauthorized user will include a limited amount of objects, after an account is created then no filters will be applied to the Space List.
