Near Earth Objects

A list of current space objects that have the closest orbit.

Astro Dodge provides a way for users to find and track Near Earth Objects (NEOs) using an intuitive interface. One of its main features is the ability for users to create watchlists of objects that they are interested in tracking. By adding an entry to their watchlist, users can track recent NEOs that pass within a certain distance of Earth or are otherwise of scientific interest.


Astro Dodge has implemented an authorization system, where unauthorized users have access to a limited amount of objects in the Space List. However, after creating an account, users can access the full Space List without any filters applied. To ensure the reliability and stability of the application, comprehensive testing has been performed, and a high level of test coverage has been achieved.
